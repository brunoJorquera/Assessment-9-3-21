About Belton,Tx




Attractions


Belton is a small town located in Central Texas that is surrounded by a large body of water and many nature trails. As far as food is concerned, Belton has many Barbecue and Grill locations to choose from as well as a large variety of foods from around the world.

If you are looking for a fun family-oriented environment, Belton is the place!

  • Mosaic Grill

  • Narunyas

  • The Gin at Nolan Creek
